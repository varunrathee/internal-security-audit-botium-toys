# 💳 PCI DSS Mapping

This document maps audit findings to key PCI DSS requirements
related to the protection of cardholder data.

---

## Requirement 7: Restrict Access to Cardholder Data
**Finding:** ❌ Not met  
All employees have access to internally stored cardholder data.

---

## Requirement 3: Protect Stored Cardholder Data
**Finding:** ❌ Not met  
No encryption is used for stored credit card information.

---

## Requirement 4: Encrypt Transmission of Cardholder Data
**Finding:** ❌ Not met  
Data is transmitted internally without encryption.

---

## Requirement 8: Identify and Authenticate Access
**Finding:** ❌ Not met  
Password policies do not meet minimum complexity standards,
and no centralized password management system exists.

---

## 📌 Summary
Botium Toys does not meet critical PCI DSS requirements and
is at high risk of non-compliance penalties and data breaches.
