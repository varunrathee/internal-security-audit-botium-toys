# 🇪🇺 GDPR Mapping

This document maps audit findings to General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
requirements related to EU customer data protection.

---

## Article 5: Data Protection Principles
**Finding:** ⚠️ Partially met  
Data integrity and availability are addressed, but confidentiality is weak.

---

## Article 32: Security of Processing
**Finding:** ❌ Not met  
Lack of encryption and access controls increases exposure of personal data.

---

## Article 33: Breach Notification
**Finding:** ✅ Met  
A plan exists to notify EU customers within 72 hours of a breach.

---

## Article 30: Records of Processing Activities
**Finding:** ❌ Not met  
No formal data classification or inventory is maintained.

---

## 📌 Summary
While Botium Toys has breach notification procedures,
insufficient technical and administrative controls
limit overall GDPR compliance.
