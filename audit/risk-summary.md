# ⚠️ Risk Summary

This document summarizes the overall cybersecurity risk posture of Botium Toys
based on the internal security audit.

---

## 🔢 Overall Risk Rating
**Risk Score:** 8 / 10 (High Risk)

---

## 🧠 Key Risk Factors
- Lack of asset classification and inventory
- No encryption for sensitive customer data
- Excessive access to internal systems
- Absence of backups and disaster recovery plans
- Missing intrusion detection capabilities

---

## 💥 Potential Impact
- Data breaches involving customer PII and payment data
- Regulatory fines due to non-compliance
- Business disruption caused by system failures
- Reputational damage and loss of customer trust

---

## 📌 Conclusion
Without the implementation of critical security controls,
Botium Toys remains highly vulnerable to cyber threats and
compliance violations.
